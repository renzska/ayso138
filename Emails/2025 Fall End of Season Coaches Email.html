📊 Player Evaluations – Help Us Build Balanced Teams

As we close out the season, please take a few minutes to complete your player evaluations for every child on your roster.

These evaluations are one of the most important things you do as a coach after the final whistle. They help us create balanced teams next season—ensuring each team has a fair mix of developing, intermediate, and advanced players. Balanced teams make for better games, happier players, and a stronger overall experience for everyone involved.

Now that we have the player evaluations being sent out prior to seasons' end, as long as we get them filled out, we'll be able to do a much better job at balancing teams for Spring 2026.               

The evaluation emails were on October 12, 2025, and have a subject of [Team Name] [Division] Player Evaluations. Instructions for filling them out are in the email. Please try to have them submitted within a few days of your final game.
