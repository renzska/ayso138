Hey Adult Volunteers!

We’ve had a bunch of you mention how fun it would be to have a casual adult soccer game for our AYSO volunteers; just a lighthearted match to get out, move around, and enjoy the game we help bring to the kids every season.

Whether you’ve played before or not, this is meant to be a fun way to connect with other volunteers. We’ll have refs to keep things flowing smoothly, but the focus is on having fun while our kids cheer us on from the sidelines.

Please take a moment to fill out this short survey so we can pick the best day and time:

Volunteer Soccer Game Interest Survey

Once we have enough responses, we’ll pick a date and send out the final details. Bring water, good vibes, shin guards, and your favorite pair of cleats or sneakers.

Thank you all for being a huge part of what makes AYSO Region 138 such a great community.

John Rennemeyer
Coach Administrator
