This is a one-time email for AYSO Region 138 Soccer past and present volunteers. If you were never a volunteer for AYSO Soccer, please disregard this email.

Hey Past and Present Volunteers,

If you volunteered within the past few years and still have any AYSO soccer equipment lying around, we’d love to gather it back up! Sometimes things get tucked away in a garage or shed and forgotten, so this is just a friendly reminder that you can drop off your old equipment tonight (Thursday, September 11, 2025).

Equipment Drop-Off

  • When: Tonight, 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
  • Where: Brigham City Sports Complex, 535 S 1200 W, Brigham City
  • What to Bring: Any old AYSO soccer balls, bags, cones, pinnies, or other coaching gear

Even if you only have a couple items, it really helps us recycle equipment and keep things running smoothly for the new season. Thank you so much for checking your trunk, garage, or storage bins and helping out!

If you volunteered this season and you've already picked up your equipment, we'll also have a drop-off opportunity on the last day of games in October.

A huge thank you to those who have coached in the past and to those coaching this season — we truly couldn’t do this without you. Your time and dedication make all the difference for the kids in our community. 💙

John Rennemeyer
Coach Administrator
AYSO Region 138
