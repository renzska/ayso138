Hey Coaches!

We're ordering coach shirts again for this season and next season, and we want to make sure everyone who has been putting in the work gets taken care of.

Here's how shirt priority works this season:

  • Returning coaches without a shirt yet — if you coached in Fall 2025 and are back this spring, you're first in line. Fill out the survey below and we'll get you squared away.
  • New coaches this spring — thank you for jumping in! Shirts for first-time coaches will go out in Fall 2026 when you return, possibly sooner depending on already available inventory. We'll have your size on file.
  • Already have a shirt — you're all set. No need to fill out the survey.

Shirts are free as a thank-you for coaching. This applies to both head coaches and assistant coaches.

Please fill out the survey by Thursday, April 23rd so we can get the order in on time.

Training Requirements

To receive your shirt, you'll need to have completed the core training requirements:

  • Background Check
  • AYSO Safe Haven
  • Concussion Awareness
  • SafeSport
  • Sudden Cardiac Arrest

Please fill out the survey even if you haven't completed the above training (please complete your training as soon as possible).

The survey includes a sizing guide to help you pick the right fit. You'll also see a few questions about other swag options. Those are just to gauge interest for this season or future seasons, so feel free to share your wishlist.
