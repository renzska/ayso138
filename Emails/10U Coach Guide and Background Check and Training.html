📘 AYSO Region 138 – 10U Game Guidelines

10U Coaches, be sure to review our 10U Guidelines that we released last season for covering some important rules and expectations:

  • ✔️ Offside Rule (build-out line replaces midfield)
  • ✔️ No Shoulder-to-Shoulder Contact
  • ✔️ Coach & Spectator Sideline Positioning
  • ✔️ Maximum Players on Field: 8v8

This is another reminder to complete your Sterling Volunteers Background Check and Training as soon as possible.

Steps to Complete Your Required Training

  1. Log in to www.ayso138.org, click My Account, then go to the Volunteertab.
  2. Identify training items with Status = None (ignore "Fingerprinting CA Only").
  3. Click on AYSOU in the left menu.
  4. In AYSOU, click Training Library.
  5. Open the Safe Haven card and complete any missing courses:
  • CDC Concussion Awareness - Online
  • Sudden Cardiac Arrest - Online
  • AYSO's Safe Haven
  • SafeSport

For your Coaching License:

  1. In Training Library, open the Coaches card (do not use Coach Instructor or CEU cards).
  2. Enroll under the division you'll be coaching. In the courses that have an In-Person component, please make sure to complete the Online portion as soon as possible. I'm working on scheduling a time to host the In-Person Course (12U).
  • As a 10U Coach, please register for the 12U Coach - Online + In-Person Course as we only host the 12U In-Person Course in Brigham City. Doing so will also get you ready ahead of time to coach 12U if you choose to do so as your child moves up through division.

Steps to Complete Your Background Check

  1. Log in to our region's website: www.ayso138.org
  2. Click on My Account (top right), then select the Volunteer tab on the left.
  3. If Risk Status shows empty or expired, check the box and click Renew & Update. Do not start a new background check if the expiration date is in the future.
  4. Confirm your information (use your full legal name) and submit.
  5. Within an hour you should receive an email from noreply@sterlingcheck.com titled "Your AYSO Background Check is Incomplete - Please submit now."
  6. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions.
