Training Reminder

Please make sure your required trainings are completed as soon as possible so you are fully cleared to coach. Core training items include:

  • AYSO Safe Haven
  • CDC Concussion Awareness
  • Sudden Cardiac Arrest (SCA)
  • SafeSport

These are mandatory for all coaches every season. If you haven’t finished them yet, please log in and complete them right away.

Player Evaluations 

During the week of our final games, you’ll receive an email with instructions for completing player evaluations. These evaluations are very important for helping us form balanced teams next season.

Now is a good time to start paying attention to how your players compare with other players across your entire division. We’ll send the official evaluation form later, but starting to think about rankings now will make the process much easier.

Thanks for all you do to support our players and families. See you on the pitch!

Field Use and BE United Update

BE United has not been fully honoring the field sharing agreement we put in place last spring, and the city will no longer share upcoming schedules with us. This means there is a chance you may occasionally overlap with a United team or the independent BC Storm comp team playing a game during practices.

Our regional commissioner is actively working with United’s leadership to get us back on track with a clear agreement so both sides can avoid conflicts and maximize field use. In the meantime, we will continue honoring the agreement by sticking to our assigned days. If you do encounter overlap, please share the space and adjust to open areas as needed.

If conflicts become too frequent while this is being worked out, we are prepared to pay to reserve practice fields directly with the city. At $30/hour per field, it’s a significant cost, so it benefits everyone if BE United and AYSO can follow through on an agreement and share the space properly.

If you hit a conflict:

  1. Share the field and keep practice moving.
  2. Send Kevin, our Regional Commissioner, a text at 435-225-6895 with the date, field, and who you overlapped with.

Thank you for your patience and cooperation while we work to resolve this.
