🤝 Coaches + Referees = Partners in Player Development

Our referees, youth and adult, are volunteers who dedicate their time to make games safe, fair, and fun. Trained referees make for better games, and our players and parents notice the difference. While referees sometimes make mistakes (just like players and coaches do), their rulings on the field are final. Please model the AYSO spirit by treating them with respect and encouragement, never criticism, especially our youth referees, who are still learning and developing just like the players. Positive support helps us recruit, train, and retain referees, which keeps our program thriving. A simple “thank you” or a kind word before and after a match go a long way in showing appreciation and reinforcing the kind of environment we want for every child.

🙏 Thank You, Coaches!

We can’t say it enough, thank you for volunteering your time, energy, and heart to lead our players. Coaching is one of the most impactful roles in AYSO, and your efforts directly shape the experience our kids have on the field. Whether it’s teaching soccer skills, modeling good sportsmanship, or cheering for every child, you make a lasting difference in their development and enjoyment of the game. We truly appreciate all that you do!
